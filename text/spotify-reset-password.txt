Resetting Your Spotify Password

If you can't remember your Spotify password or want to change it:

1. Go to the password reset page: https://www.spotify.com/password-reset/
2. Enter your email address or username.
3. Check your email inbox for the password reset link.
4. Follow the link to create a new password.
