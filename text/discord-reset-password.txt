How to Reset Your Discord Password

1. Open the Discord app or website.
2. On the login screen, click "Forgot your password?"
3. Enter the email address associated with your account.
4. Check your inbox for the reset link.
5. Follow the instructions to set a new password.

Note:
- If you didn’t receive the email, check your spam folder.
- If you can’t access your email, contact Discord Support for identity verification.

Stay secure by using a strong, unique password and enabling two-factor authentication (2FA).
