Resetting Your Steam Account Password

If you have forgotten your Steam account password or are unable to sign in, follow these steps:

1. Visit the Steam Support page: https://help.steampowered.com/
2. Click on "I can't sign in."
3. Select "I forgot my Steam Account name or password."
4. Enter your account name or the email address associated with your account.
5. Follow the on-screen instructions to receive a password reset email.

Important:
- Make sure to check your spam or junk folder if you do not receive the email.
- If you no longer have access to your email, click on "I no longer have access to this email address" and follow the alternative verification steps.

Stay secure by enabling Steam Guard and keeping your email up-to-date.
